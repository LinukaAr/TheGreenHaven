Protect, restore, and promote the healthy use of land and natural areas. Manage forests responsibly, fight against the spread of deserts, stop and fix land damage, and prevent the loss of plant and animal species.
A healthy life on land is essential for our survival on this planet. We are all part of the Earth's ecosystem, and we have caused significant harm through deforestation, destruction of natural habitats, and land degradation. Using our ecosystems sustainably and protecting biodiversity isn't just a cause; it's crucial for our own survival.
THE TARGETS THINGS TO DO

THE-TARGETS

Everyone can help to make sure that we meet the Global Goals. Use these twelve targets to create action to protect and restore life on land.

Protect Forests

Conserve and restore forests to prevent deforestation and promote reforestation.

Combat Desertification

Take measures to prevent and reverse desertification, ensuring that land remains productive.

Halt Land Degradation

Stop and reverse land degradation, ensuring sustainable land management practices.

Conserve Biodiversity

Protect and conserve natural habitats to preserve biodiversity and prevent species extinction.

Sustainable Land Use

Promote sustainable use of terrestrial ecosystems to maintain their health and productivity.

Support Local Communities

Encourage and support local communities to use natural resources sustainably and preserve their environment.

THINGS TO DO

  • Plant Trees and Reforest: Participate in or support tree-planting initiatives to restore and expand forest areas.
  • Reduce Deforestation: Use products made from sustainable sources, support policies that protect forests, and avoid activities that contribute to deforestation.
  • Adopt Sustainable Farming: Support and practice sustainable agriculture methods that maintain soil health, reduce chemical use, and prevent land degradation.
  • Protect Natural Habitats: Advocate for the protection of natural habitats and support conservation efforts to maintain biodiversity.
  • Reduce Waste and Recycle: Minimize waste by recycling and composting, reducing the strain on landfills and promoting a circular economy.
  • Educate and Raise Awareness: Educate others about the importance of sustainable land use and biodiversity conservation, and participate in community efforts to protect the environment.
  • Support Wildlife: Donate to or volunteer with wildlife conservation groups.
  • Urban Planning: Promote green spaces and sustainable city planning
